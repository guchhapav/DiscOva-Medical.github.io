Answers that flow.

Because trips to the gynecologist should give us power, not pain.

Our problems, from infertility to endometriosis, are met with constant medical misdirection and painful, invasive procedures. At Discova, we’re on a mission to reimagine health for all menstruating people, from your first period, to your last.

Your period can power the gyn health revolution.

Naturally shed each month and overflowing with cells and diagnostic data,  your period is so much more than the waste product the world treats it as. In fact, it has been shown by researchers to give insights on a wide variety of health problems.

Whether it’s replacing your standard blood draw, making invasive diagnostics more accessible, or unlocking new insights into your menstrual health, we believe your period has the power to reshape gynecology as we know it.

The discova ecosystem enables seamless collection, testing, and interpretation of a high quality, whole sample of your period.
